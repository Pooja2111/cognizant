WELCOME TO CMR TECHNICAL CAMPUS

CMR Technical Campus was established in 2009, sponsored by CMR Technical Education Society with a vision and mission of imparting quality technical education to the deserving and meritorious students. The institute is located in semi urban area on Hyderabad-Nagpur highway, about 20 km from Secunderabad and well connected through Outer Ring Road (ORR). The institute has a sprawling campus of 10 acres with lush greenery.
